Joshua Owide

Founder

Joshua (“Josh”) Owide holds a BSc in Physiology and Pharmacology. Over the past 20 years, he has worked as a pharmaceutical industry analyst, with commentary featured across a range of media outlets, and has contributed as an author to industry publications, including Deloitte’s Return on Pharmaceutical Innovation.

His motivation to build Mito Laboratories stems from a long-standing focus on mitochondrial biology that began during undergraduate research into apoptosis, and evolved into a conviction that mitochondria-centric programs can open differentiated therapeutic opportunities in areas of high unmet need.
