About us

Modern oncology has delivered major advances, yet many therapeutic strategies still focus primarily on genomic drivers or immune modulation. In parallel, a growing body of evidence reinforces that metabolic state — and mitochondrial function in particular — can be a decisive determinant of cell fate, tumour behaviour, and treatment response.

Mito Laboratories is being built around a simple conviction: a mitochondria-centric view of disease can open new therapeutic options, especially in indications where outcomes remain poor and current approaches are insufficient.

We develop protocolised, small-molecule intervention frameworks designed to apply coordinated pressure across mitochondrial, metabolic, microbial, and immune biology. Our aim is to translate under-explored and under-capitalised scientific insights into measurable programs with clear milestones — progressing from mechanistic validation to indication-led development.

That is what Mito Laboratories stands for: rigorous, systems-level biology, operationalised into investable therapeutic programs.

Our mission

Build mitochondria-focused therapeutic programs that are measurable, rational, and clinically translatable.

Our approach

Protocolised, small-molecule frameworks designed for biological complexity and clear readouts.

Our focus

Indications with high unmet need, where metabolic control may shift outcomes.
